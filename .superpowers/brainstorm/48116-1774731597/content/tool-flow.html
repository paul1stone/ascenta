Strategy Breakdown — Tool Flow

How the user interacts with the tool from start to finish

User Flow
1
User selects "Strategy Breakdown" tool in Compass
Tool appears in Compass tool selector alongside existing tools. User can also just ask "break down our strategy for me" in chat.
2
AI fetches strategy context
Tool calls getStrategyBreakdown which pulls:
• Company-wide strategy goals from Strategy Studio
• Department goals for the user's department
• User's role & department info
• (Optional) User's personal goals from Grow
3
AI explains strategy in conversation
Conversational breakdown tailored to the user's role:
• IC view: "Here's what the company is focused on, here's your department's priorities, and here's how your work connects."
• Manager view: "Here's the company strategy, here's your department's goals, and here's how your team's work maps to these."
4
User asks follow-up questions
Natural conversation — user can dig into specific goals, ask about priorities, clarify how things connect. No time limit on this phase.
5
AI offers to generate strategy brief
After the conversation, AI suggests: "Want me to put this together as a strategy brief you can download?" User confirms.
6
Document opens in working document panel
50/50 split — chat on left, read-only strategy brief on right.
New action: "open_strategy_document" block type.
Document is formatted HTML, not a form — no editable fields.
7
User downloads the brief
Download button at the top of the panel. Generates a clean PDF of the strategy brief. User can also ask AI to revise sections before downloading.
