Performance Reviews Tab — UI Layout

What the manager sees when they click the "Performance Reviews" tab under Grow

Two Entry Points

Manager-Initiated
Manager tells Compass "start a review for Sarah" or clicks "Start Review" button in the table. Compass calls startPerformanceReview.
System-Prompted
When a review period approaches (e.g., Q1 ending), a banner appears at the top of the tab: "Q1 reviews are due — 3 of 5 direct reports haven't been started." Links to start each.

Key UI Behaviors

Start Review (button click)
Sends a pre-filled message to Compass: "Start a performance review for [employee] for [period]". Triggers the chat-guided flow.
Continue → (resume in-progress)
Reopens the working document at the current step. Compass re-loads context and picks up where the manager left off.
Period Selector
Dropdown to view past review periods. Table filters to show reviews for that period. Default: current/most recent period.
Nudge Banner
Appears when review period is within 2 weeks of due date and not all reviews are finalized. Shows count of remaining reviews.
