Single Source of Truth

Managers see all direct reports in one view — no jumping between HRIS, spreadsheets, and email. Employee status, goals, and check-in history are always current.

Attention Signals

Overdue check-ins turn rose, PIP employees get a subtle row tint, and the "Needs Attention" stat card aggregates all flags. Managers can spot issues without digging.

Quick Actions

Each row has inline actions — view profile, start a check-in conversation, or open the more menu for corrective actions, goal creation, and performance notes. One click to act.

Goal Visibility

Goal progress is shown inline with a compact progress bar, giving managers an at-a-glance view of how each report is tracking without navigating to the full performance system.
