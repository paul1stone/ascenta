Character Comparison
What each pairing brings to the Ascenta brand
Option 1 — Plus Jakarta Sans + Geist
Warm geometry — rounder than Montserrat, friendlier without losing professionalism
SaaS pedigree — widely adopted by Linear, Vercel, and modern product tools
Geist body text — purpose-built for developer/product UIs with excellent monospace companion
Best for: Balancing trust and modernity for enterprise HR buyers
Option 2 — Outfit + DM Sans
Softer edges — more approachable than geometric alternatives, inviting tone
Human-centered — DM Sans has a warm, humanist quality great for people-focused products
Excellent weight range — both fonts offer smooth weight transitions for hierarchy
Best for: Making corrective action workflows feel less intimidating for HR managers
Option 3 — Sora + Geist
Tech-forward precision — distinctive letterforms with a futuristic edge
AI-native feel — reinforces Ascenta's positioning as an AI-first platform
Strong differentiation — less common than Jakarta/Inter pairings, more memorable
Best for: Leaning into AI identity and standing out from traditional HR tools
▲

All Google Fonts

Every option uses free Google Fonts with excellent language support and performance. No licensing concerns.

●

Same Design Tokens

Font swap only — all colors, spacing, shadows, and component structure remain identical. Drop-in replacement.

★

How to Evaluate

Focus on headings at different sizes, body text readability, and how labels/badges feel. These are the most common text patterns in Ascenta.
