Strategy as Measurement

Not tracking KPIs — tracking whether people understand and act on the strategy. Are goals connected? Are reviews referencing priorities?

Actionable Insights

Every gap comes with a suggested action. Ascenta doesn't just report problems — it recommends next steps like coaching resources or cascading sessions.

Not a Scoreboard

Ascenta avoids OKR tracking or finance reporting. This dashboard answers: "Is our strategy landing in how people work?" — the human side of execution.
