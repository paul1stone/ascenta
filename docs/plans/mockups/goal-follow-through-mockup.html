●

AI-Generated Milestones

AI analyzes the goal's type, time period, and success metric to generate 3-6 concrete steps with progressive difficulty and realistic timelines.

●

Chat-Driven Refinement

Users refine the plan through natural conversation — "add a step," "push this back," "make milestone 3 more specific." The AI updates the form in real-time.

●

Persisted & Tracked

Action plans are saved to the database, linked 1:1 to goals. Employees can update milestone status over time as they work through the plan.
